Why Should You Rely On Restoration Pros?

When your home or business has been critically damaged by water or a flood, it is important to seek help immediately for trusted emergency water restoration. We guarantee we will respond as quickly as possible to begin the process of flood cleanup, extraction, and drying.

When you choose Restoration Pros, our team will:

  • Perform a completely free visual inspection with moisture meters and infrared cameras
  • Identify the source of your water leak and stop it
  • Create a comprehensive action plan to get your home or business clean and dry
  • Move, pack out or block furniture to prevent it from being further damaged
  • Extract all excess moisture and water from wood flooring or carpet
  • Apply anti-microbial to prevent mold growth
  • Begin the drying process using the latest air movers and commercial grade dehumidifiers
  • Monitor moisture levels daily to ensure complete and thorough drying
  • Reconstruct all materials, flooring, drywall, and ceilings in your home
